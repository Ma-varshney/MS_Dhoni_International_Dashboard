# 🏏 MS Dhoni: The Legend’s Era – Career Analytics Dashboard

## 1. Project Title / Headline
**MS Dhoni: The Legend’s Era – International Career Analytics Dashboard** A dynamic and interactive Power BI visualization tool designed to explore the illustrious 15-year career of "Captain Cool"—focusing on performance trends, scoring consistency, and format-wise statistics from 2004 to 2019.

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose
The MS Dhoni Career Statistics Dashboard is a data-driven tribute and analytical report that visualizes over **17,200 international runs** across all formats (ODI, T20I, and Test). It provides cricket analysts and fans an intuitive way to track Dhoni's evolution as a batsman, his boundary-hitting patterns, and his year-on-year contribution to Indian cricket.

---

## 3. Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:
* 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Main platform for report creation and data visualization.
* 📂 **Power Query** – Used for cleaning and transforming raw career data.
* 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Implemented for complex measures like career averages and dynamic strike rates.
* 📝 **Data Modeling** – Established relationships to enable seamless cross-filtering between formats and years.
* 📁 **File Format** – `.pbix` for the development file and `.png` for dashboard previews.

---

## 4. Data Source
* **Source:** Historical Cricket Databases (Public datasets like ESPNcricinfo / Kaggle).
* **Structure:** The dataset contains a detailed breakdown of MS Dhoni’s international matches, including runs scored, balls faced, innings played, and milestones (50s/100s) categorized by year and match format.

---

## 5. Features / Highlights

### 🏏 Business Problem
In sports journalism, raw statistics often fail to show the "bigger picture" of a player's impact. Key questions like: *In which years did Dhoni peak?* *How did his strike rate vary across formats?* and *What was his boundary contribution?* are difficult to answer without an interactive visual narrative.

### 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard
To deliver an interactive visual journey that:
1. Analyzes the consistency of MS Dhoni across 15+ years.
2. Segregates performance by Test, ODI, and T20I formats.
3. Visualizes scoring patterns (boundaries vs. singles) and milestone achievements.

### 🔍 Walkthrough of Key Visuals
* **Performance KPIs (Top Right):** Instant view of Total Innings (525), Total Runs (17,266), Average (42.77), and Strike Rate (90.33).
* **Year & Format Slicers (Top Center):** Interactive filters that allow users to drill down into specific career phases or formats.
* **Boundaries Analysis (Donut Chart):** A visual split between Sixes (19.4%) and Fours (80.6%), highlighting his finishing style.
* **Runs by Year (Bar Chart):** A year-wise distribution of runs to identify his most productive seasons (e.g., his peak years around 2008-2009).
* **Hundreds and Fifties (Area Chart):** A trend line showing the frequency of his half-centuries and centuries over his career timeline.

### 📈 Business Impact & Insights
* **Performance Benchmarking:** Can be used by sports analysts to compare modern-day finishers against Dhoni’s career benchmarks.
* **Fan Engagement:** Provides a "ready-to-consume" visual for media houses during cricket retrospectives.
* **Strategic Analysis:** Helps in understanding how a player’s role and strike rate evolve as they progress from a youngster to a veteran.
